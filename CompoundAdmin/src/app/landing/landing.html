This is a simple compound interest calculator that allows you to calculate the future value of an investment based on various parameters such as initial investment, interest rate, contribution frequency, and more. It also considers inflation and tax on interest earnings to provide a more accurate final balance. The calculator is designed to be user-friendly, with clear labels and input fields for each parameter. It provides real-time feedback on the results after the form is submitted, displaying the total years, contributions, interest earned (both before and after tax), final balance, and inflation-adjusted balance. Your data will be securely stored in the database, and you can access it anytime.
